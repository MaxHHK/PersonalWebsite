The Power of Small Steps

True progress is often invisible daily. It's the compounding interest of tiny, consistent efforts that yields monumental results over a year.

Idea Prioritization

Learning to say 'No' to a good idea to say 'Yes' to a great one. Focus isn't about capacity; it's about disciplined elimination.

System Over Goals

My happiness this year wasn't tied to reaching targets, but to building reliable systems. The process is the reward, not the outcome.

Deep Work Time

Scheduled blocks for distraction-free work were the highest leverage activity. Protecting time is protecting value.
